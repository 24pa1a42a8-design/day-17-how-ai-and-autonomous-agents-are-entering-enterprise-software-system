# Answers

## What is Agentforce?

Agentforce is Salesforce's platform for building AI-powered agents that can automate tasks and interact with users.

---

## What is Agentblazer Champion 202?

Agentblazer Champion 202 is a learning milestone focused on advanced Agentforce concepts and practical implementation.

---

## What is Agentforce DX?

Agentforce DX provides development tools and workflows for creating, testing, and deploying AI agents.

---

## What is the Agentforce Vibes Extension?

The Agentforce Vibes Extension enhances the development experience by providing AI-assisted tools and productivity features.

---

## Why customize agents with Apex?

Apex customization allows developers to add custom business logic and integrate Agentforce with Salesforce processes.

---

## What are Agent Actions?

Agent Actions are tasks that an AI agent can perform in response to user requests.

---

## Benefits of Agentforce?

- Automation
- Increased productivity
- Improved customer experiences
- Faster issue resolution
- AI-powered assistance

---

## What skills are required for Agentforce development?

- Salesforce Administration
- Apex Development
- Flow Builder
- Salesforce DX
- AI Agent Configuration
